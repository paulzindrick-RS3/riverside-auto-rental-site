One Location, All of Western Wisconsin

Why Drivers Across the Region Come to Us

We operate from a single location because we don't need multiple lots to serve the region well. Black River Falls sits at the intersection of WI-54 and I-94 — which means drivers from Tomah, Sparta, Neillsville, Warrens, Melrose, and Osseo can all reach us within 30 minutes. That's by design. It's why we've been a western Wisconsin institution since 1983.

If you're not sure whether we're the right option for your area, call 715-396-1466 and we'll tell you honestly how long the drive is and whether it makes sense for your situation.

What We Offer Across the Region

  • Cars from $55/day — Intermediate and full-size cars with local (100 mi/day) or unlimited daily rate options.
  • 7-passenger minivans — $78/day + $0.10/mi, or $400/week unlimited. Ideal for family travel and small group trips.
