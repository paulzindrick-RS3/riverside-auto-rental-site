Locations We Serve

One pickup location on Highway 54 — within 30 minutes of most communities in western Wisconsin.

Riverside Auto Rental has served drivers across western Wisconsin since 1983. Our location at N5856 WI-54 in Black River Falls — right off I-94 at Exit 116 — puts us within easy reach of most of the region.

We regularly rent cars, minivans, and 15-passenger vans to customers from Tomah, Sparta, Neillsville, Warrens, Melrose, Osseo, and many smaller communities across Jackson, Monroe, Clark, and Trempealeau counties. For most of these drivers, we're the closest independent rental option — and the only provider in the area offering 15-passenger vans.
