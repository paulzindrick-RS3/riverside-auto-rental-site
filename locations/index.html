One Location, All of Western Wisconsin

We operate from a single location because we don't need multiple lots to serve the region well. Black River Falls sits at the intersection of WI-54 and I-94, which means drivers from Tomah, Sparta, Neillsville, Warrens, Melrose, and Osseo can all reach us within 30 minutes. That's by design — it's why we've been a western Wisconsin institution since 1983.
