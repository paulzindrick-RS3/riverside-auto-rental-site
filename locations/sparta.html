Serving Sparta, Fort McCoy & the Elroy-Sparta Trail

We've rented into the Sparta area for four decades. A few of the reasons Sparta drivers end up at our lot:

  • Fort McCoy — Sparta is a bedroom community for the Fort McCoy military installation just east of town. Reservists, active-duty personnel on temporary duty, and visiting families regularly need a reliable vehicle — and our 15-passenger vans handle unit and group travel a sedan can't.
  • Sparta Butterfest (June 11–14, 2026) — Sparta's signature festival fills Memorial Park each June, with a parade, carnival, softball tournament, and a Military Night. Groups coming in for the weekend often need a van — reserve early, they book fast.
  • Elroy-Sparta State Trail — More than 60,000 cyclists a year ride the nation's first rail-trail through Sparta. Cycling clubs and groups traveling together use our vans to shuttle riders and gear.
  • Deke Slayton Museum & historic downtown — Sparta's Space & Bicycle Museum and National Register four-corners district draw steady visitor traffic — travelers who sometimes need a vehicle while in the area.
  • Body shops & adjusters — When a Sparta-area vehicle is in for collision repair, Monroe County adjusters know to send drivers our way for a fast replacement.
