Meet Paul

Fortune 500 thinking. Local business heart.

Paul Zindrick spent years in marketing at some of the biggest retail brands in the country — Target, Best Buy, Kroger. He learned how the best companies in the world think about customers, search, and brand presence. Then he took all of that and applied it to a family business.

Eastside Urgent Care was invisible online. Competitors with bigger budgets were showing up first. Paul built their digital presence from scratch — SEO, Google Business Profile, local content — and watched the phones start ringing again. That experience changed everything.

He started Black Dog Consulting because he saw how many local businesses were getting sold expensive, complicated services by agencies that didn't understand their world. Plumbers don't care about "algorithm updates." They care whether their phone rings. HVAC owners don't need a dashboard. They need to know their trucks are rolling.

Black Dog exists to be the advisor that a small local business owner actually wants — someone who shows up, tells the truth, and delivers results you can measure in booked jobs and answered calls.
