Our Story

Serving Black River Falls for Over 40 Years

Riverside Auto Rental is a family-owned business that has been part of the Black River Falls community since 1983. We operate alongside Riverside Auto Sales at the same location on Highway 54 — two separate businesses united by a shared commitment to honest, dependable service.

Over four decades, we've built our reputation one rental at a time. Locals trust us when their car is in the shop. Insurance adjusters and body shops count on us for fast, reliable replacement vehicles. And travelers passing through western Wisconsin choose us for the personal touch you won't find at a national chain.

Our fleet includes intermediate cars, full-size cars, 7-passenger minivans, and the 15-passenger vans that set us apart from every other rental provider in the area. Whether you need a vehicle for a day, a week, or longer — we're here to help.
