Daily vs. Weekly Car Rental Rates: Which Saves You More?

A straightforward breakdown of our pricing options — so you pick the one that fits your trip and budget.

One of the most common questions we get at Riverside Auto Rental is: "Should I rent by the day or by the week?" The answer depends on how long you need the vehicle and how far you're driving. Here's the full breakdown.

Understanding our daily rate options

For daily rentals, we offer two mileage tiers for cars:

  • Local Rate (100 miles/day limit) — Our most affordable daily option. Best if you're staying in the Black River Falls area, running errands, commuting to work, or making short trips around Jackson County.
  • Unlimited Miles Rate — No mileage cap. Best if you're road-tripping, driving to Eau Claire, La Crosse, the Twin Cities, or anywhere beyond the local area.

Weekly rates — where the real savings kick in

If you need a vehicle for 7 days or more, the weekly rate is almost always the better deal. Why? Two reasons:

  1. Lower per-day cost — The weekly rate works out to significantly less per day than even the local daily rate.
  2. Unlimited mileage included — Every weekly rental includes unlimited miles at no extra charge. No tracking, no per-mile fees, no surprises.

Which rate should you choose?

Here's a quick guide:

  • 1–3 days, staying local → Daily local rate ($55–$65/day)
  • 1–3 days, driving long distances → Daily unlimited rate ($75–$85/day)
  • 4–6 days → Call us at 715-670-0209 to compare daily vs. weekly for your exact situation
  • 7+ days → Weekly rate — always the best value

Not sure which option fits your trip? Just call us — we're happy to walk through the math with you and find the best rate for your needs.
